Types of Goods

Understanding how demand responds to income and prices

Why Classify Goods?

Goods are classified in economics based on how their demand responds to changes in income and prices of related goods.

This classification helps businesses predict consumer behaviour and design appropriate pricing and marketing strategies.

Normal Goods

Normal goods are goods whose demand increases when consumer income increases, and decreases when income falls.

Most goods consumed in everyday life fall under this category.
